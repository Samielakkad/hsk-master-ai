## The insight

Most language apps chase casual learners with streaks and gamification. The real money and the real *intent* sit somewhere else: the student who **failed the HSK and has to retake it** — for a scholarship, a visa, a university place, or a job. They know exactly what they need (a higher score), they have a deadline, and they will pay for a tool that gets them there.

**HSK Master AI** is built for that person — not the dabbler, the retaker.

> The **HSK (汉语水平考试)** is the standard Chinese-proficiency exam: **40M+ attempts a year** worldwide, typically **USD 100–400** per sitting, gating scholarships, admissions and work visas across China's study-abroad pipeline.

*Written from experience — I sat the HSK myself (HSK 4, 90/100) on the way into a Mandarin-taught master's, so I know exactly where the existing tools fall short.*

## The product — three surfaces, one weak spot each

| Surface | What it does | Why it's hard for incumbents |
|---|---|---|
| ✍️ **Writing diagnostics** | Character-level error trace — not "wrong", but *which stroke, radical, or grammar pattern* failed, mapped to the exact HSK band | Requires modelling the writing system, not just grading text |
| 🗣️ **Speaking lab** | Prosody + tone scoring against native reference, targeting the spoken HSK (HSKK) | Tone is where Western learners lose the most points and get the least feedback |
| 🔁 **Spaced-rep vocabulary** | Review scheduled against the **official HSK frequency bands**, so study time maps directly to what the exam tests | Generic SRS decks aren't aligned to the exam's vocabulary tiers |

## The wedge

1. **Highest-intent moment.** The retaker has a deadline and a clear goal — the best customer in all of language learning.
2. **Exam-aligned, not vibes-aligned.** Every feature maps to a scored HSK dimension, so progress is measurable in the only metric that matters: the band score.
3. **China study-abroad pipeline.** The same audience needs admissions, scholarships and visa support — an adjacent surface ([UNICN](https://github.com/Samielakkad) and related work) that this funnels into.
